Strategic Design

Strategic Design focuses on aligning your system’s architecture with your business’s needs. By leveraging tools like Context Maps and identifying Core Domains, you can ensure that your software supports your goals effectively.

What is Strategic Design?

Unlike Tactical Modeling, which deals with the details of entities, aggregates, and value objects, Strategic Design focuses on **high-level architectural decisions**. It ensures your system is structured to:

  • Support your Core Domain and business priorities.
  • Encourage collaboration and independence between teams.
  • Manage complexity in large systems.
