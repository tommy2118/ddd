Distillation

Distillation is the process of separating the Core Domain from less critical domains, ensuring your team focuses efforts on areas that drive innovation and competitive advantage.

What is Distillation?

In Domain-Driven Design, distillation involves clarifying and separating different areas of your system based on their importance and role. The goal is to focus resources and innovation on the Core Domain while handling Supporting and Generic Domains efficiently. Key aspects include:

  • Prioritizing the Core Domain: Distinguishing areas that provide the most value to your business.
  • Identifying Supporting Domains: Recognizing complementary areas that are necessary but not differentiating.
  • Using Generic Solutions: Leveraging standard tools for common functionality.

Distillation provides clarity on where to innovate and where to optimize.

Steps for Effective Distillation

Follow these steps to distill your system into Core, Supporting, and Generic Domains:

1. Classify Domains

Analyze each domain in your system to determine whether it is Core, Supporting, or Generic. Focus on business value and uniqueness.

Example: At Kalkomey, the LMS is Core, while Marketing Tools are Generic.

2. Focus Innovation on the Core

Invest resources in the Core Domain to drive differentiation and competitive advantage. Avoid over-investing in Supporting or Generic Domains.

Example: Enhance LMS features like compliance tracking to meet regulatory demands.

3. Simplify Supporting and Generic Domains

Use off-the-shelf solutions or outsource less critical domains. Avoid over-engineering these areas to reduce costs and complexity.

Example: Use a SaaS platform for email marketing rather than building custom tools.

Example: Distillation at Kalkomey

At Kalkomey, distillation ensures that we prioritize the LMS as our Core Domain while efficiently managing Supporting and Generic Domains. Here’s how we approach it: 

Core Domain: Learning Management System (LMS)
- Responsibilities: Manage courses, learner progress, and certifications.
- Focus: Innovation and differentiation to meet regulatory needs.

Supporting Domain: E-Regulations
- Responsibilities: Maintain updated regulations for compliance.
- Focus: Efficient updates and integration with the LMS.

Generic Domain: Marketing Tools
- Responsibilities: Campaigns and analytics.
- Focus: Leverage SaaS solutions to minimize effort and cost.

This approach ensures Kalkomey remains focused on its Core Domain while simplifying operations in other areas.
