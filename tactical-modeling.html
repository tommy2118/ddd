Tactical Modeling

Tactical Modeling is where the details of Domain-Driven Design come to life. It focuses on building domain models that are rich, expressive, and aligned with the business’s rules and workflows.

What is Tactical Modeling?

Tactical Modeling focuses on the design of domain objects and their interactions within a single Bounded Context. Key elements include:

  • Entities: Objects with a unique identity that persist over time.
  • Value Objects: Immutable objects that define a concept with no identity.
  • Aggregates: Clusters of domain objects managed as a single unit.
  • Domain Events: Notifications of significant domain occurrences.
  • Repositories: Interfaces for accessing and persisting domain objects.

These tools allow developers to model the domain in a way that reflects real-world rules and workflows.
