Understanding Bounded Contexts

Bounded Contexts are the foundation of Domain-Driven Design (DDD). They help manage complexity by isolating domain models and providing clear boundaries for workflows and terminology. Mastering Bounded Contexts is the first step toward implementing DDD at Kalkomey.
